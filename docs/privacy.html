Mindful Bump Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 22, 2026

Mindful Bump is a mindfulness buffer app built around Screen Time and Family Controls on iPhone. This policy explains how the current version of the app handles data.

1. What Mindful Bump collects

In the current version of Mindful Bump, we do not collect or upload personal information from the app to our own servers.

The app is designed around on-device use. The current app experience does not use third-party advertising SDKs, third-party analytics SDKs, or cross-app tracking.

2. Information stored on your device

Mindful Bump may store app data locally on your device so the app can work as intended. This can include:

  • your selected apps or categories for buffering
  • your breathing and affirmation preferences
  • exempt period settings
  • reminder preferences
  • local usage summary data and buffer completion history
  • local subscription state cached on the device for app behavior

3. Permissions

Mindful Bump may request the following permissions:

  • Screen Time / Family Controls: used to let you choose apps or categories to buffer and to support the interception flow.
  • Notifications: optional; used for reminder notifications and to help bring you back into the app after the shield flow.

4. Purchases and subscriptions

Mindful Bump Pro subscriptions are sold through Apple's App Store using StoreKit. Apple processes billing and payment information under Apple's own terms and policies. We do not receive your full payment card details.

5. Data sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We do not share your app data for advertising or tracking in the current version of Mindful Bump.

6. Data retention and deletion

Because the current app experience is designed for local storage, the data described above remains on your device unless you delete it, remove the app, or use in-app data clearing controls where available.

7. Children's privacy

Mindful Bump is not intended as a kids-category app. If you believe the app is handling data in a way that raises a privacy concern, contact us at the email below.

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy if the app's features or data practices change. The latest published version at this URL will be the current version.

9. Contact

For privacy questions, contact lyu3239@163.com.

Mindful Bump 隐私政策

生效日期：2026 年 3 月 22 日

Mindful Bump 是一款围绕 iPhone 的 Screen Time 与 Family Controls 能力构建的正念缓冲应用。本页面说明当前版本 App 的数据处理方式。

1. Mindful Bump 收集什么信息

在当前版本中，Mindful Bump 不会把 App 内的个人信息上传到我们自己的服务器。

当前 App 体验以设备本地使用为主，不使用第三方广告 SDK、第三方分析 SDK，也不做跨 App 追踪。

2. 保存在你设备上的信息

为了让 App 正常工作，Mindful Bump 可能会把以下数据保存在你的设备本地：

  • 你选择要缓冲的 App 或类别
  • 呼吸与确认话术偏好
  • 豁免时段设置
  • 提醒设置
  • 本地使用总结数据与缓冲完成记录
  • 用于 App 行为判断的本地订阅状态缓存

3. 权限说明

Mindful Bump 可能会请求以下权限：

  • Screen Time / Family Controls：用于让你选择需要缓冲的 App 或类别，并支持拦截流程。
  • 通知权限：可选，用于提醒通知以及在 shield 流程后帮助你返回 App。

4. 购买与订阅

Mindful Bump Pro 订阅通过 Apple App Store 和 StoreKit 销售。账单和支付信息由 Apple 按其自己的条款和政策处理。我们不会收到你的完整银行卡信息。

5. 数据共享

我们不会出售你的个人数据。在当前版本中，我们不会把 App 数据用于广告或追踪共享。

6. 数据保留与删除

由于当前 App 以本地存储为主，上述数据会保留在你的设备上，直到你删除它们、卸载 App，或者在 App 内使用可用的数据清理功能。

7. 儿童隐私

Mindful Bump 不是 Kids 分类应用。如果你认为 App 的数据处理方式引发了隐私问题，请通过下方邮箱联系我们。

8. 政策更新

如果 App 功能或数据处理方式发生变化，我们可能会更新本政策。此 URL 发布的最新版本即为当前有效版本。

9. 联系方式
