Frequently Asked Questions

What is Mindful Bump?
Mindful Bump is a Screen Time based mindfulness buffer app. It helps you pause before opening selected apps or categories by asking you to complete a short breathing or affirmation step.

How do I start using it?
Open the app, grant Screen Time / Family Controls permission, then choose the apps or categories you want to buffer.

Why did the shield not jump directly back into the app?
If notifications are disabled, you may need to switch back to Mindful Bump manually after tapping Start Buffer. This matches the current system behavior of the shield flow.

Does Mindful Bump require an account?
No. The current version of Mindful Bump does not require account sign-in.

How do I restore purchases?
Open the Pro page in Mindful Bump and tap Restore Purchases to resync your subscription status with Apple.

How do I manage or cancel a subscription?
Subscriptions are managed through your App Store account. On your device, open App Store subscription settings to manage or cancel your subscription.

What does Mindful Bump Pro unlock?
Mindful Bump Pro currently unlocks custom breathing duration, custom affirmation text, unlimited app/category management, monthly trend view, and local report export.

常见问题

Mindful Bump 是什么？
Mindful Bump 是一款基于 Screen Time 的正念缓冲 App。它会在你打开指定 App 或类别前，要求你先完成一次简短的呼吸或确认话术步骤。

如何开始使用？
打开 App 后，先完成 Screen Time / Family Controls 授权，然后选择你想要缓冲的 App 或类别。

为什么点击 Start Buffer 后没有自动回到 App？
如果通知权限未开启，点击 Start Buffer 后你可能需要手动切回 Mindful Bump。这是当前 shield 流程下的系统行为。

Mindful Bump 需要登录账号吗？
不需要。当前版本不要求登录账号。

如何恢复购买？
打开 Mindful Bump 的 Pro 页面，点击“恢复购买”，即可和 Apple 重新同步你的订阅状态。

如何管理或取消订阅？
订阅通过你的 App Store 账户管理。你可以在设备上的 App Store 订阅设置中管理或取消订阅。

Mindful Bump Pro 解锁什么？
Mindful Bump Pro 当前可解锁自定义呼吸时长、自定义确认话术、不限数量管理 App/类别、月度趋势视图和本地报告导出。
